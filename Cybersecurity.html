    Cybersecurity Details

    Required Skills

    Programming, Network Security, Vulnerability Assessment, Cryptography, Risk Management

    Focus on continuous learning and certifications.

    Career Opportunities

    Security Analyst, Penetration Tester, InfoSec Manager, SOC Analyst, Cybersecurity Consultant

    Demand is increasing globally in both public and private sectors.

    Common Tools

    Kali Linux, Wireshark, Metasploit, Nessus, Burp Suite

    Mastering tools is essential for threat analysis.

    Common Threats

    Phishing, Malware, Ransomware, DDoS Attacks, Zero-day Exploits

    Staying updated with latest threats is crucial.

    Popular Certifications

    CompTIA Security+, CEH, CISSP, OSCP

    Certifications enhance credibility and job prospects.

    Work Environments

    Government Agencies, Tech Companies, Banks, Healthcare, Freelance Consulting

    Cybersecurity roles exist across various industries.
